Premium barbering in the heart of the City of London. The Kingsmen Barbers is not merely a barbershop — it is a sanctuary for the modern gentleman, where traditional craft meets contemporary luxury.

Exclusively limited to 100 members, we ensure each gentleman receives the undivided attention and bespoke service befitting his station. From the moment you step through our doors, every detail has been considered — from the hand-stitched leather chairs to the complimentary single-origin espresso.

Our team of master barbers have honed their craft across the finest establishments in London, Milan, and New York. Each appointment is an experience designed to restore, refine, and rejuvenate.
