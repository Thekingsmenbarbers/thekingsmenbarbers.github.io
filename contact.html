Getting Here

Directions Through the Passage

From Leadenhall Market

  1. Enter Leadenhall Market from Gracechurch Street through the main covered arcade.
  2. Walk through the market towards the western end, passing the restaurants and shops on your left.
  3. Look for Ship Tavern Passage — a narrow Victorian alleyway leading south off the market, adjacent to Bull's Head Passage (the one from Harry Potter).
  4. Walk through Ship Tavern Passage. Number 12 is on your right — look for the brass door handle.
  5. Step inside. Take a seat. You've arrived.

By Public Transport

  1. Bank station (Central, Northern, Waterloo & City lines) — 5-minute walk east along Cornhill and Leadenhall Street.
  2. Monument station (Circle, District lines) — 4-minute walk north through Gracechurch Street to Leadenhall Market.
  3. Liverpool Street station (Central, Circle, Elizabeth, Hammersmith & City, Metropolitan lines + National Rail) — 8-minute walk south-west.
  4. Fenchurch Street station (National Rail) — 3-minute walk west.
